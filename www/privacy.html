Privacy Policy

How we protect your information

Introduction

DIY Paradise ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information we collect from you. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

By accessing our website, services, or providing us with your personal information, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

At DIY Paradise, we collect and manage user data according to the following policy:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Order History and Preferences: Details of your purchases and preferences for our products and services.
  • Usage Data and Website Interaction: Information about how you use our website and services.
  • Device and Browser Information: Details about the devices and browsers you use to access our website.

We collect personal information by lawful and fair means and ensure it is relevant for the purposes outlined below.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our website.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our products and services.
  • To understand and analyze how you use our website.
  • To communicate with you about promotions, updates, and news.
  • To respond to inquiries or customer support requests.
  • To process transactions and deliver orders.
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our legal rights.

3. Disclosure of Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share it with:

  • Service Providers:Third-party providers that help us deliver our services, such as payment processors, shipping partners, or IT support.
  • Legal and Regulatory AuthoritiesWhen required by law or to respond to lawful requests.
  • With Your ConsentWhen you provide explicit consent for us to share your information.

4. Storage and Security of Personal Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. These steps include:

  • Implementing encryption and secure servers for sensitive data.
  • Regularly updating and reviewing our security systems.
  • Restricting access to personal information to authorized personnel only.

5. Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct your personal information held by us. If you wish to do so, please contact us using the details below. We may request proof of identity to process your request.

6. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies allow us to:

  • Track how you interact with our website.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our offerings.
  • Personalize content and services based on your preferences.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Cross-Border Disclosure

In some cases, we may store or process your information outside Australia. If so, we ensure these overseas entities comply with the Australian Privacy Principles or equivalent standards.
