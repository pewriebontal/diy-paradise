About DIY Paradise

Building Dreams, Empowering DIYers

Our Story

Empowering Australians to transform their homes and spaces through quality tools, expert guidance, and innovative solutions.

Since our establishment in 1985, DIY Paradise has been at the forefront of Australia's home improvement industry. What began as a small hardware store in Newcastle has grown into a nationwide network of stores, supported by our cutting-edge online platform.

Our Values


  • Quality First: We source only the finest tools and materials.
  • Customer Success: Your project's success is our priority.
  • Sustainability: Committed to eco-friendly practices and products.
  • Innovation: Continuously evolving to meet modern needs.
